# Project Summary Report Format

## Metadata
- title: "Project Summary Report"
- max_words: 2500
- date_format: "YYYY-MM-DD"

## Section Weights
- Project Overview: 0.15
- Objectives & Scope: 0.15
- Current Status: 0.20
- Key Milestones: 0.15
- Stakeholders & Resources: 0.10
- Risks & Dependencies: 0.15
- Next Steps: 0.10

## Structure

### Project Overview
Comprehensive description of the project including:
- **Project Name**: Official name and any code names or abbreviations used
- **Project Description**: 2-3 paragraph detailed explanation of what the project aims to achieve
- **Business Context**: Why this project exists, what problem it solves, and its strategic importance
- **Project Type**: Classification (new development, enhancement, migration, integration, etc.)
- **Start Date**: When the project was initiated
- **Target Completion**: Expected delivery date or phase completion dates
- **Project Sponsor**: Executive or business owner championing the project
- **Project Lead**: Day-to-day responsible party

### Objectives & Scope
Detailed breakdown of project goals:

**Primary Objectives**:
- List each major objective with measurable success criteria
- Include quantifiable targets where possible (e.g., "reduce processing time by 40%")
- Specify which objectives are must-have vs nice-to-have

**Scope Definition**:
- **In Scope**: Explicit list of what is included in this project
- **Out of Scope**: Explicit list of what is NOT included (important for managing expectations)
- **Scope Changes**: Any approved changes to original scope with dates and rationale

**Success Criteria**:
- Specific, measurable conditions that define project success
- Acceptance criteria for deliverables
- Performance benchmarks or SLAs to be met

### Current Status
Detailed status assessment:

**Overall Health**: [GREEN/YELLOW/RED] with explanation
- GREEN: On track, no significant issues
- YELLOW: Minor issues or risks requiring attention
- RED: Significant issues threatening timeline, budget, or scope

**Progress Summary**:
- Percentage complete (overall and by phase/workstream)
- Work completed in current reporting period
- Comparison to planned progress (ahead/on-track/behind)

**Budget Status**:
- Total budget allocated
- Amount spent to date
- Projected final cost
- Variance explanation if over/under budget

**Schedule Status**:
- Original timeline vs current timeline
- Days ahead or behind schedule
- Critical path status

**Recent Accomplishments**:
- Bullet list of completed items in the reporting period
- Key decisions made
- Approvals obtained

**Current Blockers & Issues**:
- Active issues preventing progress
- Severity and impact of each blocker
- Mitigation actions being taken
- Escalation status

### Key Milestones
Chronological milestone tracking:

| Milestone | Target Date | Actual Date | Status | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|--------|-------|
| [Name] | YYYY-MM-DD | YYYY-MM-DD | Complete/In Progress/At Risk/Delayed | Details |

For each milestone include:
- Clear definition of what constitutes completion
- Dependencies on other milestones
- Impact if milestone is missed
- Owner responsible for delivery

**Upcoming Milestones** (next 30-60 days):
- List with dates and readiness assessment

**Completed Milestones**:
- Summary of achieved milestones with any lessons learned

### Stakeholders & Resources
**Key Stakeholders**:
| Name/Role | Organization | Interest Level | Influence Level | Communication Frequency |
|-----------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| [Name] | [Org] | High/Medium/Low | High/Medium/Low | Weekly/Bi-weekly/Monthly |

**Project Team**:
- Core team members with roles and allocation percentages
- External consultants or vendors involved
- Resource gaps or needs

**Resource Utilization**:
- Current team capacity vs demand
- Planned resource changes (additions, departures)
- Skills gaps identified

### Risks & Dependencies
**Active Risks**:
| Risk ID | Description | Probability | Impact | Risk Score | Mitigation Strategy | Owner |
|---------|-------------|-------------|--------|------------|--------------------| ------|
| R001 | [Description] | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | [P x I] | [Strategy] | [Name] |

For each risk include:
- Trigger conditions (what would cause this risk to materialize)
- Early warning indicators
- Contingency plan if risk materializes
- Date identified and last reviewed

**Dependencies**:
| Dependency | Type | Owner | Status | Impact if Delayed |
|------------|------|-------|--------|-------------------|
| [Description] | Internal/External | [Team] | On Track/At Risk | [Impact] |

- Internal dependencies (other projects, teams, systems)
- External dependencies (vendors, regulatory, market conditions)
- Critical path dependencies highlighted

### Next Steps
**Immediate Actions** (next 1-2 weeks):
- Prioritized list of tasks with owners and due dates
- Decision points requiring stakeholder input
- Meetings or reviews scheduled

**Upcoming Phase/Sprint**:
- Goals for next planning period
- Key deliverables expected
- Resources required

**Decisions Needed**:
- List of pending decisions with decision makers and deadlines
- Options being considered
- Recommended course of action

**Communication Plan**:
- Scheduled stakeholder updates
- Escalation triggers
- Reporting cadence
