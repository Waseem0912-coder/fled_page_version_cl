# Ticket Summary Report Format

## Metadata
- title: "Ticket & Issue Summary"
- max_words: 2000
- date_format: "YYYY-MM-DD"

## Section Weights
- Overview & Statistics: 0.15
- Critical Issues: 0.25
- Issue Categories: 0.20
- Resolution Status: 0.15
- Trends & Patterns: 0.15
- Action Items: 0.10

## Structure

### Overview & Statistics
Reporting period and volume metrics: opened, closed, currently open, backlog. Priority distribution with resolution times. SLA performance metrics. Source breakdown of tickets.

### Critical Issues
Detail each high-priority open issue: ID, title, status, severity, age, assigned team. Describe symptoms, affected scope, business impact. Current investigation status, workarounds, blockers, estimated resolution.

### Issue Categories
Break down by issue type (bug, feature, performance, security, etc). Break down by component/system. Break down by team ownership. Identify recurring issues and their root cause status.

### Resolution Status
List recently resolved tickets with resolution type and time. Summarize resolution categories. Provide aging analysis of open tickets. Flag stale tickets needing follow-up.

### Trends & Patterns
Volume trends (week-over-week, month-over-month). Quality indicators (escape rate, regression rate). Top root cause categories. Correlations with deployments or changes.

### Action Items
Immediate actions with owners and due dates. Short-term process improvements. Required escalations. Follow-up review schedule.
