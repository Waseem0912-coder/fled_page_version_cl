# Executive Summary Report Format

## Metadata
- title: "Executive Briefing"
- max_words: 3000
- date_format: "YYYY-MM-DD"

## Section Weights
- Executive Summary: 0.10
- Situation Overview: 0.15
- Root Cause Analysis: 0.25
- Impact Assessment: 0.25
- Current Situation: 0.10
- Final Action & Recommendations: 0.15

## Structure

### Executive Summary
Brief leadership briefing: what happened, severity, status, key decision needed. Include critical metrics.

### Situation Overview
Describe what occurred, when, how it was discovered. Provide chronological timeline of key events. Include context, scope of affected systems/users/regions.

### Root Cause Analysis
Identify primary cause with evidence and confidence level. List contributing factors. Explain the causal chain from trigger to impact. Note what controls failed and what worked.

### Impact Assessment
Quantify business impact: revenue, cost, productivity loss. Measure operational impact: availability, performance, error rates. Describe customer impact: affected count, experience degradation. Note any compliance, legal, or reputational concerns.

### Current Situation
Current status and stability. Active mitigations and workarounds. Ongoing activities and resource allocation. Risk of recurrence assessment.

### Final Action & Recommendations
Immediate actions (0-48hrs) with owners. Short-term actions (1-4 weeks). Long-term initiatives. Preventive measures. Decisions required from leadership. Success metrics and follow-up schedule.
